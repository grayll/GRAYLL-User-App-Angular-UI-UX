GRAYLL | Stellar Network Account

Your GRAYLL account holds all your GRX and XLM (Lumens) on the Stellar Network.

Federation Address (Payment Alias)

Your federation address is your payment alias so people can send you assets more easily.

Changing your GRAYLL alias also changes your network payment alias (aka your federation address).
You can use this alias, including the *grayll.io, instead of your public key (Stellar Network Wallet Address) to receive GRX and XLM payments on the Stellar Network to your GRAYLL account wallet.

Stellar Address (Public Key)

The Stellar wallet address is the public key associated with this account.

Secret Key

Your secret key is hidden and must not be shared. Anyone that has your secret key has access to your wallet and the assets.
